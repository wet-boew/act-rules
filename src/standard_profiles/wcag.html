Level AA (#levelAA)

Conformance at level AA include all success criteria (SC) tagged at level AA and at level A.

## Level A (`#levelA`) Conformance at level A include all success criteria (SC) tagged at level A.
## WCAG delta 2.1 (`#delta21_levelAA`) Conformance at delta 2.1 level AA include all succes criteria added to WCAG 2.1 since WCAG 2.0 at level AA and level A.
